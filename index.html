Hello!

We're Lacunae, Newberry College's student-produced
magazine of literary, scholarly, and artistic works.

Lacunae are empty spaces or missing parts. Every student
has words that go unspoken, and we offer the opportunity
to piece together many voices to create a
beautiful harmony.
